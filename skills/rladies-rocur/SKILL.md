# WeAreRLadies Rotating Curation (RoCur)

[`@weare.rladies.org`](https://bsky.app/profile/weare.rladies.org) on **Bluesky** features
a different R-Lady each week. A curation runs **Monday 11:00 UTC → Saturday 16:00 UTC**.
Admins (a rotating team of 1–4, monthly) recruit curators, schedule them, hand over
account access, and support them. Write all comms in the
[RLadies+ voice](../rladies-brand/SKILL.md).

## Who can curate

- Must **identify as a gender minority**; must be **in the RLadies+ directory** (sign up
  if not); must be an **individual** (a chapter can't curate, but an organizer can).
- No RLadies+ affiliation required. Aim for diversity of profession, experience (novice
  to expert), location, and language (any language; English not required).
- **Repeat curation** allowed if **≥2 years** since the last; new curators get priority.

Forms: sign-up <https://rladies.org/form/rocur-signup/> · schedule
<https://rladies.org/form/rocur-schedule/> · nomination (Airtable, auto-emails the
nominee).

## Admin timeline (per curator)

The recurring job. Each step has an Airtable checklist to update and (for most) an email
template — all verbatim in
[references/admin-workflow.md](references/admin-workflow.md).

1. **On sign-up** — add them to the schedule promptly; save their photo to the Google
   Drive as `YYYY-MM-DD-firstname` (week's Monday); email **confirmation** (Email 1).
2. **On nomination** — Airtable auto-emails; if manual, send the **invitation** (Email 2).
3. **1 week before** — draft their intro graphic from the **Canva RoCur brand template**
   (Brand ▸ Brand templates ▸ RoCur); email it for approval + prep info + guide link +
   optional Buffer (Email 3); set up Buffer with **Approval Required** if they want it.
4. **Sunday before** — switch Buffer to Full Posting Access if used; **remove the previous
   curator's Bluesky app password and generate a new one** named `firstname-lastname`
   (**never tick "Allow access to your direct messages"**); email it (Email 4); post the
   intro graphic with alt text before 18:00 UTC.
5. **Just before** — set the account avatar to their photo and add their handle to the bio.
6. **After** — remove them from Buffer; reset avatar to the WeAreRLadies logo and bio to
   "This week's curator: TBA"; add them to the starter pack; send **thank-you** (Email 5).

There's no pressure to fill every week — admins may schedule hiatus weeks.

## Access & security

Curators log in at Bluesky with username `weare.rladies.org` + the **app password** you
email them. Rotate it every week (remove old, generate new). Access is revoked the
following Sunday; remind them to sign out.

## Curating agreement (what curators sign up to)

Follow the CoC; be the sole poster; use **inclusive language** ("all", "folks", "y'all" —
not everyone identifies as female); **no promoting businesses/services or paid content**;
don't change the display name/avatar/banner/bio; don't follow/unfollow/block or DM
(except blocking an abuser, then alert `weare@rladies.org`); add alt text to images. The
full agreement, the curator **pre-curation checklist**, posting tips, and the Bluesky/
Buffer FAQ are in [references/curator-guide.md](references/curator-guide.md).

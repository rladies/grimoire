# RoCur curator guide: checklist, agreement, posting & FAQ

Deep reference for `rladies-rocur`. Sources: `rocur/guide`, `rocur/faq`. For curators
(and the admins supporting them).

## Pre-curation checklist (do ~1 week before)

1. Post from your personal Bluesky account.
2. If using Buffer, log in and get familiar; see Buffer's "using Bluesky with Buffer".
3. Post an image **with alt text** from your personal account (practice).
4. Read at least one artifact from a previous curator.
5. Brainstorm a list of topics you hope to post about — most curators find this helps.

## Curating agreement (verbatim points)

1. Abide by the RLadies+ Code of Conduct in all activity on the account.
2. You're responsible for all content during your week; admins act on harmful content.
3. No obscene or abusive language.
4. **Use inclusive language** — "all", "everybody", "y'all", "folks" — not everyone is
   female.
5. Be the **sole poster** during your week.
6. **Do not promote or advertise any business**, or receive payment to do so.
7. Don't change the account's display name, avatar, banner, or bio unless asked.
8. Don't follow/unfollow/block users or DM. (If abused, you may block the offender, then
   alert `weare@rladies.org` with the handle + a description.)
9. Access is revoked at the end of your week.
10. Submit a profile picture and short bio before curating.

Promotion is "broadly defined as any instance where any entity receives financial
remuneration from the Bluesky post" (referral links, paid courses, "buy this book").
**Allowed**: how you use R at work, open-source packages, and free/openly-available
resources.

## Posting tips

- **Introduce yourself** first (work, how you use R, hobbies, links).
- Be as active as you can (no fixed count). Keep it relevant to R and/or RLadies+, and
  useful for learning.
- **Images**: add **alt text** (and to GIFs); get permission and tag owners; event photos
  are encouraged.
- **Links**: link packages to CRAN/Bioconductor/GitHub; mention maintainers' Bluesky
  unless very well known.
- **Hashtags**: use `#rladies`; use `#rstats` when broadly useful (don't overuse); also
  `#dataviz`, `#rspatial`, `#opendata`, package names, your chapter location.
- **Engage**: reply to people (a quote post lets others see the original); ask the
  community questions.
- **Sign off** with a last post: note the week's ending, a key takeaway, thanks, and where
  to follow you.

## FAQ highlights

- **Log in**: Bluesky web/app with username `weare.rladies.org` + the emailed app
  password; **New Post** to start.
- Up to 4 photos, or a GIF, or a video per post. Delete via the post's `...` → Delete.
- **Alt text**: the **+ ALT** button at the top-right of an uploaded image.
- **Polls**: poll.blue (use the `weare.rladies.org` handle). **Pin**: `...` → Pin to your
  profile (unpin before your week ends).
- **Buffer** (if granted): Publish tab → WeAreRLadies Bluesky channel → **+ New Post** →
  before your week, **Save as Draft**; during, **Add to Queue / Share Now / Share Next /
  Schedule Post**. Limits (as of Apr 2025): can't reply from Buffer, no GIF search, no alt
  text on GIFs, no @-handle autocomplete — drafting in Buffer then posting in Bluesky is
  often better for rich media.

Further questions: `weare@rladies.org` or #rocur on the Community Slack.

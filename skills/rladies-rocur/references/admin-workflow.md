# RoCur admin workflow & email templates

Deep reference for `rladies-rocur`. Source: `rocur/admin guide`. Update the **RoCur
Curator Sign Up Airtable** checklist after each step. Photos: shared Google Drive,
named `YYYY-MM-DD-firstname` (the curation week's Monday). Intro graphics: Canva **Brand
▸ Brand templates ▸ RoCur**.

## App password rotation (Sunday before)

Remove the previous curator's app password (Bluesky **Settings → Privacy and security →
App passwords →** trash icon), then **Add App Password** named `firstname-lastname`.
**Do not select "Allow access to your direct messages."** Email it (Email 4).

## Buffer (optional)

Invite (1 week before): profile icon confirms RLadies+ org → **+ New → Invite a Team
Member → Invite a New User** (name + email) → **Admin Access: No** → assign the **Bluesky**
channel at **Approval Required**. Sunday before: change to **Full Posting Access**. After:
**Team →** find them **→ Edit User → Remove**.

## Email templates (verbatim)

**Email 1 — sign-up confirmation**

```
Subject: Your opportunity to curate for the We Are RLadies+ Bluesky account

Hi [curator's name],

Your curation is scheduled to begin on [start date of curation]. We'll contact you
1 week before your curation with more details. Let us know if you have any questions!

Thank you,
Rotating Curation (RoCur) admins
```

**Email 2 — nomination invitation** (if not auto-sent)

```
Subject: Invitation to become a curator on the We Are RLadies+ Bluesky account

Hi [potential curator's name],

My name is [your name], and I'm an admin for the We Are RLadies+ Rotating Curation
(RoCur) account on Bluesky (https://bsky.app/profile/weare.rladies.org). RoCur is a
program where we feature a new curator every week from the R community.

You were nominated by [nominator's name] to be a curator because [reason from form].
If you're interested, please fill out this form: https://rladies.org/form/rocur-signup/
More details: https://guide.rladies.org/rocur/about/

Thank you,
RoCur admins
```

**Email 3 — graphic approval + prep (1 week before)**

```
Subject: Request for approval of graphic for We Are RLadies+ Bluesky account

Hi [curator's name],

We're delighted to have you curate next week! You are scheduled to curate from Monday,
[start date] to Saturday, [end date]. We can modify this slightly (e.g., start Tuesday
or end Friday) — just let us know.

1. We've attached the graphic we'll post this Sunday to introduce you. Please let us know
   any changes no later than this Wednesday.
2. If you'd like to use Buffer (https://buffer.com/) to schedule your posts, let us know
   by Wednesday — we'll send an invite. This is optional.
3. Please read the guide for curators: https://guide.rladies.org/rocur/guide/
4. If you haven't, add yourself to the directory: https://rladies.org/directory/

We'll contact you later this week with details on accessing the account.

Thank you,
Rotating Curation (RoCur) admins
```

**Email 4 — app password (Sunday before)**

```
Subject: App password for the We Are RLadies+ Bluesky account

Hi [curator's name],

Your curation starts Monday, [start date] at 11:00 AM UTC and ends Saturday, [end date]
at 4:00 PM UTC.

Your app password to log into the We Are RLadies+ Bluesky account is [app password].
See the FAQ for how to log in: https://guide.rladies.org/rocur/faq/
Curating agreement & tips: https://guide.rladies.org/rocur/guide/#curating-agreement

Questions? Email us or join #rocur on the RLadies+ Community Slack.

Looking forward to your curation!
Rotating Curation (RoCur) admins
```

**Email 5 — thank you (after)**

```
Subject: Thank you for contributing to the We Are RLadies+ Bluesky account!

Hi [curator's name],

Thank you so much for curating! Please remember to sign out of the account.

Your feedback helps us improve the program — what went well, what could be better? Just
reply to this email. If you wrote about your week and want it in the guide, send a PR or
let us know. And please consider nominating fellow RLadies+ to curate.

Thank you for your service to the RLadies+ community!
Rotating Curation (RoCur) admins
```

## Intro post (Sunday, before 18:00 UTC)

```
This week's #RLadies curator is [their Bluesky handle].

Alt text: Curator [name], [role/title] at [organization]. They say, "[what they do in R]".
```

After the week: reset avatar to the WeAreRLadies logo (saved in the Google Drive root),
set the bio's last line to "This week's curator: TBA", add the curator to the starter
pack.
